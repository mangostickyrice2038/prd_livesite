Mission

Philanthropy Research Desk facilitates and improves transparency, engagement and access to capital for funders and for-purpose organisations. We make the ecosystem easier to navigate so aligned partners can collaborate sooner and deliver greater social impact.

Transparency

Shine a light on who funds what, where and why so values-aligned partners can connect.

Engagement

Curated introductions and tailored funder events that build trust and shared learning.

Access to Capital

Practical pathways that connect profit and purpose, moving impact investment into the mainstream.

Approach

We operationalise what we recommend. Drawing on philanthropy, CRM, project management and grant-writing expertise, we roll up our sleeves and demonstrate what good looks like inside your context.
